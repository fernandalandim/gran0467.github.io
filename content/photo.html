Photography

Photography is the art of capturing light with a camera, usually via a digital sensor or film, to create an image. Lenses matter more than any other piece of equipment because they determine what photos you can take in the first place.

The main techniques to take a good photo are:

  • Rule of Thirds
  • Framing
  • Leading lines
  • Shapes
  • Repetition

Photography gear and Softwares:

  • Canon T5 with lenses: 18-55mm, 75-300mm
  • Adobe Lightroom Classic
  • Adobe Photoshop
  • Adobe Premiere Pro
